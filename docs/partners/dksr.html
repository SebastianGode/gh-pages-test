DKSR – Open-Source Data Platform

Shape the future of your municipality efficiently and sustainably via strategic data usage made possible with the open urban platform of the Data Competence Center for Cities and Regions (DKSR). Operated on the Open Telekom Cloud, the AI powered data platform serves municipalities and municipal enterprises as a basis for sovereign and data-secure digitalization.

Product type: PaaS

Municipal Urban Data Platform

Digitalization is not an end in itself. Instead, it provides municipalities with the opportunity to develop data-based solutions to current challenges, to generate synergistic effects via networking with one another, and to design their citizen's living space in an efficient and sensible way, both in the short and long term. DKSR provides a base infrastructure for this, laying the foundation for data-supported urban development. The scope of services ranges from the mapping of existing data, over harmonization and aggregration, to their provision to solution providers.

Data sovereignty

With DKSR and the Open Telekom Cloud, you always have full control over your own data. The technical implementation of this is achieved using pragmatic solutions from the fields of edge, cloud, and artificial intelligence.

Bundling of competencies

Via the bundling of competencies in the management of urban data, with DKSR you benefit from extensive expert know-how in the fields of urban data and smart cities & regions.

Community Management

In addition to its open source data platform, with DKSR you benefit from a strong community for knowledge exchange and joint implementation of digital solutions for municipal partners.
