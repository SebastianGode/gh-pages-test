P3L Smart Learning World

Gamification as a guarantee for interactive knowledge transfer and individual, skill-dependent learning paths

Product type: SaaS, Webapp

Provider: platform3l GmbH

Website: www.platform3L.com

Digital classes that are fun and provide individual support thanks to AI

Learning paired with fun – the P3L Smart Learning World combines motivation (trivia quizzes against fellow students and teachers, avatars and medals, etc.), interactive concepts and fun for proven learning success. Since not every student is the same, the digital interactions can be tailored to support every child based on their individual skills. The P3L Smart Learning World is the perfect tool for schools and teachers to support their lessons. Moreover, there are no maintenance efforts for schools thanks to the solution’s cloud-based and GDPR-compliant deployment on the Open Telekom Cloud.

Learning made easy by playing quizzes against the computer, fellow students or through exercises

Test the students’ knowledge in different areas in quizzes against the computer or fellow students.

Gamification elements help build student stamina

Be it individual avatars, rewards for special tasks or other incentives, the P3L Smart Learning World is packed with gamification elements designed to boost student stamina.

Incognito and GDPR-compliant

Players can only be identified by their avatar and player name, meaning that no conclusion can be drawn on the actual person playing.
