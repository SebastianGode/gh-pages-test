Good Applications – We operate for good reason

twenty20 operates sustainable, common-good focused, fully managed, highly available and autoscaling web applications. Sustainable operation in Open Telekom Cloud for the common good

Digitalization can only support sustainability if it is sustainable itself. By running your application with twenty20 in the Open Telekom Cloud, you help offset the environmental and social impacts of digital operations. twenty20 invests in projects through selected partners that are committed to the common good and who make conserving resources their primary mission. The minimum goal is carbon neutrality. In the long term, regeneration is targeted to minimize negative impacts on the social foundation and ecological ceiling.

Run individual applications more sustainably

Your own customer application can also be quickly migrated to the Open Telekom Cloud and operated by twenty20. The migration is carried out by the experienced twenty20 teams. A trade-off is made in close coordination between the "lift and shift" or "cloud-native" approach, in which an application is set up with containers in the cloud container engine (Kubernetes). By applying GitOps and Infrastructure-as-Code (Terraform) principles, you remain independent at all times. Of course, twenty20 maintains the same ethical standards for your customer application in regards to sustainability and public welfare.
