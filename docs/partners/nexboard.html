neXboard

Sketch your ideas with neXboard – digitally, collaboratively and in real time. neXboard offers a digital, cloud-based whiteboard to boost your agility, creativity and flexibility.

Product type: SaaS

Provider: neXenio GmbH

Website: https://nexboard.nexenio.com/

neXenio creates solutions for easy and secure collaboration

neXenio is a young IT start-up that focuses on data security and distributed collaboration. Together with renowned partners, including the German Bundesdruckerei, SAP and HPI, the Berlin-based company develops solutions to facilitate secure collaboration. In 2019, the emergent start-up was presented with the Digital Champions Award in the category “Digital Processes & Organization.” In addition to being part of Deutsche Telekom’s TechBoost team, neXenio is also a member of the T-Systems Cloud Hub.

From analog to digital, or: offline becomes online!

Work results and ideas previously only noted down on paper can now be digitized in a matter of seconds. Just take an image of your results, upload them, and create a new digital workspace where you can document and refine your ideas – your data is secure thanks to certified hosting by Telekom.

Efficient teamwork – also when working from home

neXboard is designed to facilitate dynamic and flexible teamwork. Share the digital workspace with colleagues, partners and customers to work on your ideas in an agile manner, while visualizing the entire process in real time. Remote working has become an essential element in our work, both for global collaboration and when working from home.

Numerous method templates

Thanks to its close collaboration with design thinking coaches and workflow consultants, neXenio offers a broad range of different templates. Besides design thinking, neXboard is most commonly used in brainstorming sessions, meetings, project management, product development, and education.

Multimedia presentation and integration into the visualization process

neXboard supports many commonly used file types, from indispensable Microsoft Word and PowerPoint files to various image file formats. Even videos can be embedded without any problems!

Live chat support

neXenio puts its customers first, which is why the neXboard support team is always available to provide technical and creative advice in an uncomplicated manner.
