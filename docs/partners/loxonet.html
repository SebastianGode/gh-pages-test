Loxonet – An App for All

Employee apps are the new generation of the classic intranet. Loxonet unites all employees on an app & online platform and promotes communication, knowledge sharing, and automates administration.

Product type: App, SaaS

Provider: Loxonet GmbH

Website: https://loxonet.com/en/

From the porter to the board of directors. Finally really reach all employees. Your app. Your branding. Your future.

Loxonet GmbH is a Hamburg-based software company whose mission is to digitalise German companies and organisations. Designed for companies between 50 - 2,300 users, Loxonet centralises all core areas of communication, organisation and administration on a central platform. Loxonet is a Telekom TechBoost startup, has been on the Open Telekom Cloud since 2019, and serves companies and organisations in the DACH region.

Modern and effective communication

Organise, structure, inform - Loxonet is the complete solution for your internal communication. Put an end to paper wars and many different tools; with Loxonet, transparent management becomes child's play.

Celebrating success together

Motivate and retain employees. At Loxonet, the focus is on modern, efficient and transparent communication. Forget emails, CC and BCC - communicate project-related concerns in a group chat, via video conference or create a post on the dashboard / digital noticeboard.

Mobile, central and across locations

Whether in the office, at the club, at home or on the move, on a mobile phone, tablet or computer - you can use Loxonet from anywhere and via all available end devices. Thanks to the mobile app, teamwork has never been so uncomplicated.

Secure networking

Data security is the main priority of the Hamburg-based software company. Loxonet guarantees GDPR-compliant data protection, daily monitoring and back-ups, and much more. All services are hosted on Telekom servers.

Reliable support

Loxonet’s professional support team is available around the clock. The team is available via telephone and chat, and also on site for training/installation upon request. Every customer has their own individual contact, which means average response times range between 3 and 5 minutes.
