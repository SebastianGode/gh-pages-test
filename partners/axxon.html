Axxon VSaaS Datacenter

Axxon VSaaS Datacenter is a video management software that enables communications service providers and system integrators to build cloud-based video surveillance solutions (VSaaS) that harness the power of AxxonSoft's neural network analytics and intelligent search for recorded video.

Product type: VSaaS

Provider: AxxonSoft Europe Sp. z.o.o.

Website: https://www.axxonsoft.com/

Any Scale. Full Functionality. AI-Enabled.

AxxonSoft is a leading developer of video management and physical security information management software. Since 2003, the company has been creating disruptive technologies that push the safety & security industry forward. AxxonSoft’s unique strengths are Smart Forensic Search in recorded video and customizable video analytics powered by artificial intelligence (AI). To meet the needs of a particular facility, AxxonSoft AI neural network learns to perform customer-specific tasks from video material obtained onsite.

AI-Powered Video Analytics

Axxon VSaaS Datacenter uses AI to recognize and track abnormal behavior in complex scenes, such as suspicious individuals or vehicles. It also enables an earlier detection of dangerous conditions such as smoke or fire compared to other types of sensors, where every second counts.

Smart Forensic Search

Axxon VSaaS provides you with intelligent technologies to help you find what you need – and faster. Metadata describing video footage is simultaneously recorded and synchronized, allowing you to retrospectively use search queries to find specific moments in the recording. Just set your search criteria, and you’ll get the results within seconds.

Remote Access

Manage, configure, and operate independent systems all in the same interface using the Cross-System Client. This is especially useful for geographically distributed sites or multiple-location chains with their own surveillance systems. With Axxon VSaaS Datacenter, you have the cloud service, advanced web client and mobile apps all at the tip of your fingers.
