Flip – The employee app changing deskless work

Flip enables internal communication and connects all employees from the office to production.

Product type: App, SaaS

Provider: Flip GmbH

Website: https://www.flipapp.de/en

Click Here

Simplified communication – your personal employee app

Flip connects employees from the office to production in a mobile and smart manner. The employee app enables you to easily reach deskless workers in your company – for example, by providing non-desk workers with company news. All your employees are better integrated in the company. Flip connects and engages your employees, while also strengthening their identification with the company. Flip gives each and every one of your employees a voice and fosters teamwork. The employee app is GDPR compliant and is hosted on European servers certified in accordance with ISO-27001. The app is ideally suited for companies with a large number of deskless employees in all sectors and areas, whether production, trade and logistics, or finance and health.

Newsfeed: always up to date

Your employees can always keep an eye on all company news and updates thanks to an intuitive top-down flow of information. PDF files, images and videos can be distributed to selected employees in a targeted manner and appear in their Newsfeed. The solution also offers additional functions, such as creating internal events and surveys, or scheduling appointments – easily and in real time.

Business chat: smart shortcut for communication

Whether individual employees, departments, or the entire workforce, the Business Chat feature allows you to reach the right person – from the office to production. The feature offers individual and group chat options to foster targeted internal communication among your employees. It can be used on all mobile end devices and enables you to contact your staff in a matter of seconds. All common file formats can be sent via the chat function.

Integration: embedding existing processes

The app can easily be expanded. With our help, you can easily implement Flip in your existing IT landscape. Everything is possible: Internal HR processes, such as vacation and sick leave, can also be integrated. Flip can also be connected to existing IT systems and tools, such as Microsoft 365 or SharePoint, as well as a calendar or survey tools.

Employee directory: who’s who

Do you know your entire workforce and know which employee works in which department? Keep track of your staff with the employee directory and put a face to every name. New colleagues are listed in the directory and their area of responsibility is clearly assigned. This guarantees a transparent company infrastructure and simplifies communication.

Data and employee protection: security as top priority

Data security is very important to us. We offer the most secure method for internal communication, meeting requirements that private messenger services cannot keep up with. Flip encrypts all sensitive data in compliance with the GDPR and is hosted on European servers certified in accordance with ISO-27001. We also care about the protection of all employees. Flip was developed in collaboration with works councils to prevent monitoring based on performance and working hours. Protect your employees and prevent unnecessary working hours.
